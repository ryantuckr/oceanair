What We Do:

We’ve got everything you need to enjoy your time at the beach.

Chair and Umbrella Set

Each reservation comes with 2 adjustable beach chairs and a sun shade umbrella. The chairs are exremely comfortable and can be taken down to the water. The sun shade umbrella expands to offer a large amount of shade. We will install the sun shade at your location for you.

Paddle Boards

Stand up paddle boarding is one of the most fun activities you can get into off shore! Our stand up paddle board rentals provide a large platform from which to peer into the marine environment from above. These boards are wonderful for cruising along and checking out the shallow coral reefs. You are sure to see all kinds of colorful fish as you glide past one another.

Snorkle Gear

So you are ready to get into the water! There are so many wonderful things to look at underwater. Our rental sets feature a dry snorkle and a mask with a flexible seal, and a fin set. They are comfortable and easy to use. Our Snorkle crew will help you choose the right size.

Cabana Rental

Enjoy your own private cabana located on the most desirable location on the beach. Stay cool, relax, and enjoy the many benefits and amenities of a cabana rental. Each private cabana comes with food and drink service, extra chairs, and can accomodate up to 6 people.

Surfboard/Bodyboard Rental

Feel like taking on some waves? You can do it sitting or standing with our surfboard and bodyboard rentals.

Surf Lessons

Always wanted to learn how to surf? Sign up for a class or register for a private lesson. Our instructors will have you surfing and enjoying the waves like never before!
